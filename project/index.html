Our Process

We believe that great results come from a clear and consistent approach. At Charmorae, our cleaning process is designed to deliver the deepest clean with the highest care—every single time. From inspection to final checks, we follow a step-by-step method that ensures your furniture looks, feels, and smells as good as new.

Pre-Inspection

Assessing the fabric type, level of dirt, and any stains or damage.

Vacuuming & Pre-Treatment

Removal of surface dirt and apply stain remover

Deep Cleaning

Removes with steam or foam extraction machines dirt, bacteria, and odours.

Drying & Deodorizing

Speed-drying your upholstery and applying fabric-safe deodorizers.

Final Check

Ensure your upholstery looks and smells fresh.
